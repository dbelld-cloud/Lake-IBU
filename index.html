About Lake IBU

Nestled in the hills of Mohawk, Tennessee, Lake IBU is a scenic outdoor destination known for its peaceful waters, fishing opportunities, hiking areas, and relaxing natural beauty. Visitors enjoy kayaking, wildlife viewing, and community events hosted throughout the year.
